Our Story

The Beginning

A Workshop,
A Dream,
A Legacy

GlowCraft Studio was founded in 2018 by Priya Mehra, a jewelry designer with a background in traditional Indian goldsmithing and contemporary European design. Having trained in both Mumbai and Florence, Priya returned home with a singular vision: to create jewelry that was unmistakably Indian in soul, yet impeccably modern in form.

Her first studio was a 400-square-foot room in Bandra, Mumbai — filled with hand tools, raw gold bars, and an unshakeable belief that handcraft was not a relic of the past, but a living tradition worth preserving. She worked alone for the first two years, selling exclusively through word of mouth and a quiet Instagram presence.

Today, GlowCraft Studio is a team of eight — each a specialist in their craft. Together, they produce fewer than 500 pieces per year, ensuring that every single creation receives the full attention it deserves.

We believe jewelry is not decoration — it is identity. It is the ring your grandmother wore to her wedding, the necklace you reach for on your worst days, the earrings you passed down to your daughter. At GlowCraft, we make heirlooms.

The Craft

Made by Hand,
Finished by Fire

Every GlowCraft piece begins life as a sketch on paper — a conversation between intuition and intention. From there it moves to the bench, where metal is cut, filed, shaped, and soldered by hand.

The firing process — where raw metal meets the torch — is where a piece truly comes alive. Temperature, timing, and touch combine in a process that can only be learned through years of practice. No machine can replicate it.

Behind Every Piece

The Making of
a GlowCraft Piece

Each creation follows a sacred four-step process refined across six years of practice.

01

Design & Sketch

Every piece begins with a hand-drawn sketch — a conversation between intuition and intention. We refine until the design has a soul.

02

Material Selection

We personally select each gemstone and assay every batch of gold. No compromises. No shortcuts. Only what belongs in the piece.

03

Handcrafting

Using traditional tools — hammer, file, torch — our artisans breathe life into metal. Each piece takes between 8 to 40 hours to complete.

04

Final Inspection

Priya personally inspects and signs off on each piece before it leaves the studio. Our quality standard is singular: perfection.

What We Stand For

Our Beliefs

We don't just make jewelry. We make meaning.

01

Craft Over Volume

We deliberately limit our output. Quality is not a feature — it is the entire point. We will never scale at the cost of soul.

02

Ethical by Design

Every material we use is ethically sourced. Our gold is responsibly mined, our gemstones are conflict-free, and our waste is zero.

03

Community First

We employ local artisans, pay living wages, and invest in apprenticeship programs to keep India's goldsmith traditions alive.

04

Timelessness, Not Trend

We don't design for seasons. Our pieces are meant to be worn for decades, passed down through generations, never out of style.
