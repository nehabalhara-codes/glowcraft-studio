Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

If your question isn't answered here, please reach out directly.

01How long does a bespoke commission take?

Bespoke pieces typically take 4–10 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a precise timeline after our initial consultation and design approval.

02Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries with full insurance. International orders arrive within 7–14 business days via secure courier.

03Can I bring my own gemstone or heirloom?

Absolutely. We frequently work with inherited stones and metals. This is some of the most meaningful work we do. Book a studio consultation to discuss.

04What is your return and repair policy?

We offer complimentary repairs and polishing for the lifetime of any GlowCraft piece. Custom commissions are non-returnable, though we'll work with you until you're fully satisfied.

05What materials do you work with?

We work in 18K and 22K gold, sterling silver, and gold vermeil — all ethically sourced. We use certified conflict-free diamonds and natural gemstones exclusively.

06How do I care for my GlowCraft jewelry?

Each piece ships with a care card. In general: store separately in a soft pouch, avoid harsh chemicals, and bring it to us annually for a complimentary polish and inspection.
