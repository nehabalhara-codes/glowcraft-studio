Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

If your question isn't answered here, please reach out directly.

01 How long does a bespoke commission take?

Bespoke pieces typically take between 4–10 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a precise timeline after our initial consultation and design approval.

02 Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries with full insurance coverage. International orders are dispatched via secure courier and typically arrive within 7–14 business days.

03 Can I bring my own gemstone or family heirloom?

Absolutely. We welcome heirloom projects and work frequently with inherited stones and metals. This is some of the most meaningful work we do. Book a studio consultation to discuss.

04 What is your return and repair policy?

We offer complimentary repairs and polishing for the lifetime of any GlowCraft piece. Custom commissions are non-returnable, though we'll work with you until you're fully satisfied.

05 What materials do you work with?

We primarily work in 18K and 22K gold, sterling silver, and gold vermeil — all ethically sourced. We use certified conflict-free diamonds and natural gemstones exclusively.

06 How do I care for my GlowCraft jewelry?

Each piece comes with a care card. In general: store separately in a soft pouch, avoid harsh chemicals and perfume, and bring it to us annually for a complimentary polish and inspection.
