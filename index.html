A Glimpse Into Our World

Born from Passion,
Worn with Pride

GlowCraft Studio was born in a small Mumbai workshop where our founder, Priya Mehra, first discovered the transformative power of handcrafted gold. What began as a personal obsession became a studio revered for its quiet luxury.

Our pieces are not mass-produced. They are deliberate, emotional, and alive — crafted with the knowledge that they will be worn on the most meaningful days of someone's life.
